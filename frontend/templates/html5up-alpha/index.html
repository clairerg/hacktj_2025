Preserving Political Integrity

Created by Andrew Choi, Claire Guo, Kavya Kuttuva, and Alex Do

Introducing the ultimate tool for political engagement
Creating a more accessible future

We at PoliScope believe that the most important source
of information comes from our leader themselves. Our goal is to provide
summaries and information about your politial leadership to better inform your political descions.
By choosing PoliScope we cut down the time you need to spend reading/listening to
political speeches in order to enable you to spend more time researching key stances that are
important to you. We also provide reverse filtering to allow you to find candiates that
support similar beliefs to you.

Search for a political candidate's name to learn more about them and their political stances!
